Introduction

Posted on October 29, 2024 by 0xChimera ||

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Shim, a junior web penetration tester dedicated to strengthening web security. Alongside showcasing my work in cybersecurity, I'm excited to present: Gunmetal. my latest open-source template.

Gunmetal introduces two key elements new to my design approach: a fluid width layout and a light-on-dark color scheme. The fluid width allows the content to adapt seamlessly to different browser sizes, while the light-on-dark scheme reverses the traditional layout, featuring lighter text on a dark background for a modern, sleek look.
